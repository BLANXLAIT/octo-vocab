Octo Vocab is a privacy-first vocabulary learning app designed specifically for students in grades 7-12 studying Latin and Spanish. Built with education and privacy as core principles.

COMPLETELY PRIVATE
• Zero data collection - no accounts, analytics, or tracking
• Works completely offline - no internet required
• Your progress stays on your device
• COPPA & FERPA compliant for educational use

EDUCATIONAL FOCUS
• ACTFL-aligned leveling system
• Designed for classroom and homework use
• Perfect for individual study or group learning
• Grade-appropriate vocabulary sets

MULTI-LANGUAGE SUPPORT
• Latin vocabulary with English translations
• Spanish vocabulary with English translations
• Plans for additional languages in future updates

LEARNING FEATURES
• Smart learning queue - words you know won't reappear
• Flashcard study mode with progress memory
• Interactive vocabulary quizzes
• Progress tracking (stored locally)
• Adaptive difficulty levels

STUDENT-FRIENDLY DESIGN
• Clean, distraction-free interface
• Optimized for both phone and tablet
• Accessibility features included
• No ads or in-app purchases

Perfect for homeschool families, classroom teachers, and independent learners who value privacy and effective vocabulary study.

Open source and built by educators, for education.
